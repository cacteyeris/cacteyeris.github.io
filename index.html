About Me

Once upon a time, there was a girl named Iris. She is Ukrainian, and really likes to code, and has an odd infatuation for Pokemon and animals that look like Pokemon. One day, she murdered her friend Ken. He called her a mark-ass honky. She didn't like that. Her and her Prius, Edmund Kemper, fled. We haven't seen her since. There will be a $5 reward if she is found dead. $1 if she's alive.
